Project information:

Project for the course "Geoinformation in Society" in the winter semester 2017/2018 at ifgi, University of Münster
by Auriol Degbelo and Tomi Kauppinen.

The project deals with the visualization of unemployment data on the level of federal states in Germany. The web application gives an insightful overview about the development of unemployment in Germany in a time period of ten years (2006 to 2016). Moreover the application is enriched with the visualization of additional data
like GDP per capita or the number of industries within a federal state as a bar diagram.
